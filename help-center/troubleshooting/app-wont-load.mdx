---
title: My Glitch app won't load
description: Fix apps that fail to load or show errors.
---

## Symptoms

- App displays a loading screen endlessly
- Error: "Something went wrong!"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your logs">
    ````bash
    Log in to Glitch, open the Logs pane for your project, and look for errors.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart your app">
    Click **Tools** > **Restart App** to force your project to reboot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">If the issue persists, check for code errors in `server.js` or ask in the Glitch community.</Callout>
